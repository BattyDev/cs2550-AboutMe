My personal interests

I really love my dogs. My wife and I have two: a Jack Russell–Chihuahua mix named Opal and a Terrier mix named Bandit. We've had both for about five years now, and we love training them and taking them out for exercise. Opal is pretty great at agility courses! Unfortunately, the location closest to us with equipment went out of business recently. We're looking for other ways to keep her busy!

I'm also very into Gundam, as my "About Me" page probably makes clear. It all started with a model kit I picked up from Target one weekend as a fun project, and it quickly spiraled into TV shows, movies, more model kits, and now the trading card game. It's been a great way to keep in touch with people I may not get to see too frequently. Please do not ask me how much I've spent on Vbucks.
